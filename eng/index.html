    Biography

    Runqiong is a Ph.D. student. His research interests include cutting tool condition monitoring, intelligent manufacturing, and digital twins of manufacturing systems.

    Education and Working Experience

    • 2020.09-Now, Ph.D. Student, School of Mechanical Engineering, Shandong University
    • 2018.08-2020.08, Mechanical Engineer, Hyundai Motor R&D Center (China)
    • 2015.09-2018.01, M.E., Northeastern University (China)
