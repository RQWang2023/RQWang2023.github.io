    Runqiong Wang Ph.D. Student

    Runqiong's research interests include cutting tool condition monitoring, intelligent manufacturing, and digital twins of manufacturing systems for thin-walled parts.

    What's new

    • [2023.02.28] The paper on reinforced fusion algorithms for signal features of thin-walled parts milling has been accepted for publication in J Manuf Syst.
